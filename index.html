About Me

Hi, I'm a 20-year-old student living in Manchester. I'm in the final year of a Web Development degree, with aspirations to move into front-end development.

I've known programming for 5 or 6 years now, starting back in the early days of Minecraft modding with Java.

University began with Digital Media/Marketing – but given that what I wanted to focus on was primarily the web, I switched my degree to Web Development in my final year.

I do still enjoy marketing! I'd love the chance to apply what I learnt about digital marketing into the work that I do.
