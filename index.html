About Me

Hi, I'm a 20-year-old student living in a flat Manchester. I'm in the final year of my Web Development degree, and have aspirations to move into front-end development.

I've known programming for 5 or 6 years now, starting back in the early stages of Minecraft modding and recently moving into the web.

University started with Digital Media/Marketing, but having never really been into the marketing side, I was allowed to switch my degree to Web Development in my final year.

It fits much more with my tech background, and the work that I'm doing reflects what I'd like from my future.
