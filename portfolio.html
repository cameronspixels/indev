My Process

For some context, I'll quickly detail my approach to projects. I'll likely alter it here and there, as there's always something new to learn, or emerging technologies changing the way that we work.

Discovery

After getting in touch, we'll shortly meet in order to discuss your future website. Perhaps the most important phase, these questions will tend to inform the majority other of the later process.

We'll learn about the prosper and struggles of your digital strategy, and how this project can successfully tie into and enhance that.

Research

Using aspects identified like your audience, competitors and some of your personal preferences, I'll dive into research. This would best inform me how a website can cater towards your application and potential customers.

Completed in this phase:

  • Website audits.
  • User profiling and personas.
  • Competitor analyses.
  • Tehnical requirements.

Design

With preliminary stages complete, the product can beging to take form. Several stages of iterative design through which the product grows in fidelity, with testing phases throghout, ensure that the ideas are correct before diving into development.

Completed in this phase:

  • Low-fidelity wireframes.
  • Medium to high fidelity designs.
  • A/B user testing.
